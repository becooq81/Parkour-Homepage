We aim to solve

Under SDG 3, which focuses on Good Health and Well-being, Parkour directly addresses the unique challenges faced by Parkinson's patients, enhancing their ability to communicate and engage with digital platforms, thereby improving their quality of life and health outcomes. This innovation in healthcare technology aligns with the goal's emphasis on providing solutions tailored to specific health needs. Simultaneously, the project significantly contributes to SDG 10, Reduced Inequalities, by making digital communication more accessible to people with Parkinson's. This accessibility reduces the digital divide and promotes inclusivity, ensuring that individuals with this condition have equal opportunities to engage in the digital world. By empowering Parkinson's patients with improved communication tools, the app fosters greater social participation and helps mitigate the inequalities they face, aligning seamlessly with the goal's objective of enhancing inclusion and reducing disparities.
